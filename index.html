Elias A.Bacarro

Accounting Student & Part Time Web Developer

"The real voyage of discovery consists not in seeking new landscapes, but in having new eyes."

They say not to judge a book by its cover and as an accountant and web developer, I let the phrase ruminate in every bit of work I do. A company's website tells one story and its financial statements tell another. The powers in the hands of the people behind the screens — looking at every cell and every line of code.

From the ancient ruins in Athens to the modern PC Bangs in Seoul, I aim to see more, learn more and do more. In everywhere I go and in everything I do.
